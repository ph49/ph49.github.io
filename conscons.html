How to Play

Welcome to Consonant Constriction!

The goal is simple: Name as many things as you can in the given category.

The Catch:

You cannot use any consonants that have appeared in your previous correct guesses!

Vowels (A, E, I, O, U) can be reused as much as you like. Once a consonant is gone, it's gone for the rest of the game.
